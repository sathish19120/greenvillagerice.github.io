About us

Built on one simple belief

Every family deserves to know where their rice comes from. We started Green Village Rice because we were tired of buying rice from warehouses without knowing how old it was or where it was grown.

We work directly with paddy farmers in Nalgonda, Khammam and Warangal. We mill in small batches. We pack fresh. Every single bag has a QR code — scan it and see exactly which farm, which mill and which date it was packed. No secrets. Just fresh rice at a fair price.

Our Mission
Fresh, traceable rice for every household in Hyderabad
Our Vision
Telangana's most trusted farm-to-home rice brand by 2028
Our Values
Freshness, transparency, fair pricing, community
